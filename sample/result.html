What is Mineral Engineering?

What is a Mineral?

A Mineral is an naturally occuring inorganic substance having fixed chemical composition and structure

What is Mineral Engineering?

Mineral Engineering refers to the process of benefication of the ore using physical means to improve the grade of the ore

Why is Mineral Engineering Required?
  • To make the ore ready for subsequent metallurgical operation
  • To reduce Handling costs
